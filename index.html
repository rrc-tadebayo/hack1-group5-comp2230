Napoleonic Wars
The Napoleonic Wars (1803 to 1815) were a series of global conflicts in which various European coalitions fought against France, first as the French Republic and later under Napoleon Empire. Rooted in the French Revolution and the earlier Revolutionary Wars, they led to French dominance over much of Continental Europe.
